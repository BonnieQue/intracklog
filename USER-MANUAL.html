Getting Started

Welcome! InTrackLog is your South African vehicle mileage and expense tracker. This guide walks you through every feature so you can get the most out of the app.

What InTrackLog does

InTrackLog tracks your vehicle journeys and related expenses, then turns that data into reports you can submit to SARS for travel allowance claims, or to your employer for reimbursement.

Where to use it

  • Mobile app (Android) — Full features including GPS trip tracking. Best for daily use.
  • Web at intracklog.com — Same login, same data. Use this for managing your account, generating reports, or testing on a computer.
GPS trip tracking is only available on the mobile app, not the website.

Creating an account

  1. Open the app or go to intracklog.com
  2. Tap "Get started"
  3. Fill in your full name, email, and a password (at least 6 characters with a letter and a number)
  4. Tap Continue
  5. Add your first vehicle now or skip and add later
  6. Check your email for a verification link — click it to confirm

Signing in

  1. Open the app or website
  2. Tap "Sign in"
  3. Enter your email and password
  4. Tap Sign in — you'll land on your dashboard

Forgot your password?

  1. On the sign in screen, tap "Forgot password?"
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your inbox for a reset link (also check spam)
  4. Click the link to set a new password
  5. Sign in with your new password
02

Your First Vehicle

Before you can log trips, you need at least one vehicle on file.

Adding a vehicle

  1. From the dashboard, tap Vehicles (the car icon)
  2. Tap + Add vehicle
  3. Fill in the licence plate (e.g. "CA 456-789"), description (e.g. "Toyota Hilux 2.4 GD-6"), and current odometer reading
  4. Tap Add vehicle

Updating odometer readings

If your odometer creeps ahead of what's in the app:

  1. Tap Account → Settings → Odometer
  2. Find your vehicle and update the current reading
The dashboard automatically tracks your latest odometer reading from your trip entries — manual updates here are only needed if you skipped logging some trips.

The Dashboard

Your dashboard is the home screen of the app. Everything starts here.

What you'll see

  • Five quick-access buttons at the top: Vehicles, Trips, Locations, Reports, Teams
  • Your monthly stats: total trips, distance driven
  • Your current vehicle card
  • Recent trips and your latest odometer reading

Top right corner

Tap the InTrackLog logo at the top left to return to the default dashboard view at any time.
04

Tracking Trips

You can log trips two ways — GPS tracking (automatic) or manual entry.

Method A · GPS tracking (mobile app only)

  1. From the dashboard, tap "Start a trip"
  2. If you have multiple vehicles, choose which one you're using
  3. Allow location permissions when prompted
  4. Drive! The app tracks your route automatically
  5. When done, tap "Stop & save trip"

Your trip is saved with distance, duration, start and end locations (auto-detected via GPS), and a date/time stamp.

Method B · Manual entry

  1. Tap Vehicles → choose your vehicle
  2. Fill in the New entry form: date, odometer start & end, expense type, amount, optional notes
  3. Attach receipts or photos if you have them
  4. Tap Save entry

When to use which

ScenarioMethod
You're about to start drivingGPS — tap Start a trip
You forgot to start GPSManual entry
You don't have signal/permissionsManual entry
You want a precise route mapGPS
05

Logging Expenses

Every trip can have an expense attached, and you can log expenses without a trip too — perfect for servicing or tyres.

Expense types

  • Fuel — petrol, diesel
  • Service — vehicle servicing
  • Tyres — replacement, rotation, repair
  • Toll — e-toll, gantry
  • Other — anything else

Attaching receipts

  1. When logging an entry, scroll down to the Attachments section
  2. Tap to add a photo from your camera or pick a file from your phone
  3. The receipt is uploaded and stored with the entry
Your receipts are accessible later in Reports for SARS submissions — keep them organised here so you don't lose them.
06

Saving Locations

Frequently visit the same places? Save them as locations for quick reference and clearer reports.

Adding a saved location

  1. From the dashboard, tap Locations
  2. Tap + Add a saved location
  3. Fill in: location name (e.g. "Head Office"), address, city, country
  4. Tap Save location

Auto-discovered locations

Anywhere you start or end a GPS trip is automatically detected and added under "Discovered from trips". These help you see where you've been without manual setup.

Working with Teams

If you manage a fleet or work with colleagues who share a workplace, Teams lets you coordinate.

Teams is available on the Pro and Business plans only.

Creating a team

  1. Tap Teams on the dashboard
  2. Tap the dropdown → + Create a new team
  3. Fill in team name, workplace name, and workplace address
  4. Tap Next
  5. Choose a mileage rate: Standard (R 4.64/km), Custom, or None
  6. Tap Create team (or Skip to skip rate)

Adding members

  1. Inside your team, tap the Members tab
  2. Tap + Add
  3. Enter the member's name, email, and role (Admin or Member)
  4. Tap Add member

Sending invitations

  1. In the team view, tap the Pending tab
  2. Tap + Send invite
  3. Enter the person's email
  4. Tap Send invite — your default email app opens with a pre-written invitation
  5. Hit Send in your email app

The pending invite appears in the Pending tab until they join or you cancel.

Switching between teams

Use the dropdown at the top of the Teams section to switch between My Logbook (your personal trips), any team you belong to, or to create a new team.

08

Generating Reports

Reports are how you get your data out of the app for SARS submissions, employer reimbursement, or personal records.

Generating a report

  1. Tap Account → Settings → Generate Report
  2. Review the summary card showing total trips, distance, and expenses
  3. Choose your export format

Export formats

FormatBest for
PDF reportSARS submissions and employer reimbursement (printable, branded)
CSV (Excel)Opening in Excel/Google Sheets for further analysis
JSON data exportFull data backup including teams, locations, settings (your POPIA right)

What's in the PDF report

  • Header — your name, email, employee number (if set), date generated
  • Summary table — total trips, total km, mileage rate, reimbursement, expenses
  • Trip log — every trip with date, vehicle, plate, from/to, distance, reimbursement amount
  • Footer note — explains the report's intended use

Account & Settings

Personalise your experience and keep your account up to date.

Edit Account

Tap Account → Edit Account. Two tabs:

  • My Subscription — see and change your plan
  • Account Details — edit your full name, email, employee number (appears on reports), and change your password

Settings sections

  1. Vehicles — view and add registered vehicles
  2. Workplace — set your default workplace name and address (used in reports)
  3. Mileage Rate — set the rate per km for reimbursement (default R 4.64 — the SARS standard rate)
  4. Odometer — update each vehicle's current odometer reading
  5. Notifications — toggle reminder notifications
  6. Generate Report — export your data
11

Notifications

Reminders to keep your records up to date.

Setting up

  1. Tap Account → Settings → Notifications
  2. Toggle on what you want:
    • Daily trip reminder — 6:00 PM every day, prompts you to log today's trips
    • Monthly report reminder — 1st of every month, prompts you to generate your report
  3. Tap Send test notification to confirm they're working

To disable, toggle off in the same place. You can also disable all notifications from your device's system settings.

Privacy, Security & Data

Your data is yours. Here's how we keep it safe.

Security at a glance

  • All data stored securely in our cloud database (encrypted in transit and at rest)
  • Only you can access your data (row-level security)
  • GPS tracking is opt-in and only runs when you actively start a trip
  • We never sell your data to third parties

Reading the legal stuff

  • Privacy Policy — Account dropdown → Privacy Policy. POPIA-compliant, explains what data we collect and your rights.
  • Terms of Service — Account dropdown → Terms of Service. The legal terms of using InTrackLog.

Exporting your data

Per POPIA, you have the right to receive all data we hold about you:

  1. Go to Account → Settings → Generate Report
  2. Tap Export all my data (JSON)
  3. The file contains everything: vehicles, trips, expenses, locations, teams, settings

Deleting your account

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email privacy@intracklog.com with subject "Account deletion request". We'll remove your data within 30 days. Some data may be retained as required by law (SARS records: 5 years).

Changing your password

  • From the app: Account → Edit Account → Account Details → Change password
  • If you've forgotten it: Use the "Forgot password?" link on the sign in screen

Troubleshooting

Quick fixes for common issues.

GPS tracking won't start

  • Check that location permissions are granted: Phone Settings → Apps → InTrackLog → Permissions → Location → Allow
  • GPS only works on the mobile app, not the website
  • Restart the app after granting permissions

I added a vehicle but it didn't save

  • Make sure your subscription supports another vehicle (Free = 3, Starter = 5, Pro = 10, Business = unlimited)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try logging out and back in

The Pay button does nothing on the website

  • Make sure you're using https://intracklog.com (the secure version)
  • Some browsers block payment forms on insecure connections
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)

I'm not getting password reset emails

  • Check your spam / junk folder
  • Confirm you typed the right email address
  • Wait a few minutes — emails can take up to 5 minutes
  • Try again — request a new reset link

The app is on Free mode but I paid

  • Wait 1-2 minutes for PayFast to confirm the transaction
  • Sign out and back in to refresh
  • If still wrong, email support@intracklog.com with your transaction reference

I can't see my Teams

  • Teams require a Pro or Business subscription
  • Check Account → Edit Account → My Subscription to confirm your tier
