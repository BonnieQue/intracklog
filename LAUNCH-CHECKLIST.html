Legal & Business Setup

Must-have

These admin tasks legitimise the business. Mostly non-coding — paperwork and registrations that need to happen before going live.

Register InTrackLog (Pty) Ltd with CIPC
Privacy Policy and Terms reference a "Pty Ltd" — needs to actually exist. Online registration via CIPC eServices.
~R 175
~5 days
PayFast FICA approval
Without this, no real money can be processed. Currently waiting on PayFast verification of submitted documents.
In progress
POPIA registration with Information Regulator
Legally required for SA businesses processing personal info. Free online registration at justice.gov.za/inforeg
Free
~30 min
Designate an Information Officer
POPIA requires you name one (can be yourself initially). Documented internally and submitted with POPIA registration.
Free
Open business bank account
Required for PayFast payouts. Use FNB, Nedbank, Standard Bank, or any SA business banking provider.
~3 days
Tax clearance with SARS
Register the business for income tax. Apply at sars.gov.za
Free
VAT registration
Only required if turnover exceeds R 1m/year — defer until you're approaching that threshold.
Defer
Lawyer review of Privacy Policy & Terms
The drafts in the app are template-quality. A SA lawyer should review before public launch to ensure POPIA + consumer law compliance.
R 2K-5K

App Store Launch

Must-have

Everything required to get InTrackLog live on Google Play (and Apple App Store later).

Final app icon (replace placeholder)
10+ designs already created in icon-designs/. Pick one, convert to 1024×1024 PNG, place in assets/icon.png.
~30 min
Splash screen design
Currently using a placeholder. Should match the chosen icon. Place in assets/splash.png.
~30 min
Adaptive icon for Android
Android requires a separate foreground image (108×108dp). Place in assets/adaptive-icon.png.
~15 min
Google Play Developer account
One-time fee. Sign up at play.google.com/console. Identity verification takes 1-2 days.
$25 once
D-U-N-S Number (for Play Console org verification)
Required for Google Play organisation accounts since 2023. South Africa has no self-serve form — email dnb@transunion.co.za with CIPC certificate, physical address, postal address, and telephone number. Phone fallback: 0861 482 482 (ask for the D&B desk). Free; ~30 business days standard, ~5-8 days expedited (paid).
~30 days
Apple Developer Program (for iOS)
Annual fee. Only needed if launching on iOS. Can defer until Android has traction.
$99/year
Store listing assets
5-8 phone screenshots, 1024×500 feature graphic, short description (80 chars), full description (4000 chars), category, content rating.
~3 hours
Data Safety / Privacy declaration
Both stores require a questionnaire about exactly what data you collect (location, email, payment, etc.) and why.
~30 min

Production Readiness

Should-have

Technical pieces that aren't strictly required to launch but make the business actually run reliably once users come.

PayFast IPN webhook (Edge Function)
Critical for security — currently subscriptions update based on URL redirect. The IPN webhook verifies payments server-to-server before activating the plan.
~1 hr work
Subscription expiry handling
If a recurring payment fails (card expired, etc.), the app currently stays at the paid tier forever. Need logic to downgrade users on payment failure.
~1 hr work
iOS build
You have an Android APK. iOS requires Apple Developer account ($99/yr) and EAS build for iOS profile.
~30 min build
Error tracking (Sentry / Bugsnag)
When something breaks for a user, you currently have no visibility. Sentry has a generous free tier.
Free
~30 min setup
Production Supabase plan
Free tier limits: 500MB DB, 1GB storage, 50,000 monthly active users. Pro plan adds backups, larger storage, support.
$25/mo
Supabase Data API grants on the production project
Since 30 May 2026, new Supabase projects don't auto-expose public tables to the Data API. A fresh production project will come up "empty" (tables exist, RLS set, but every query fails) until grants are added. Run all migrations including supabase-migration-8-grants.sql, and add a GRANT under every future CREATE TABLE. Check Dashboard → Advisors → Security Advisor before 30 Oct 2026.
~10 min
Custom email sender (Resend / SendGrid)
Currently password resets and team invites come from "noreply.supabase.co" — looks like spam to most filters. Resend has 3,000 free emails/month.
Free
~1 hr setup
Analytics (PostHog / Mixpanel)
To know if users are actually using features, where they drop off, etc. Both have free tiers.
Free
Database backup strategy
Supabase Pro includes automatic daily backups. Document recovery process.
Free w/ Pro

Recommended launch path

If you want to launch within 4-6 weeks, focus on these tasks in this order.

Week 1-2
Essentials. Register Pty Ltd with CIPC. Submit POPIA registration. Wait for PayFast FICA to clear. Pick and finalise the app icon. Take 5-8 real screenshots from your phone.
Week 3-4
Store readiness. Set up Google Play Developer account ($25). Build a production AAB. Submit for internal testing first, then production. Have lawyer review legal docs.
Week 5-6
Polish & launch. Set up error tracking (Sentry free). Build the PayFast IPN webhook for secure payment verification. Set up branded email sender (Resend free). Public launch — share the Play Store link.
