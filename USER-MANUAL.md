## 1. Getting Started

### What InTrackLog does

InTrackLog tracks your vehicle journeys and related expenses, then turns that data into reports you can submit to **SARS** for travel allowance claims, or to your **employer** for reimbursement.

### Where to use it

- **Mobile app (Android)** — Full features including GPS trip tracking. Best for daily use.
- **Web (https://intracklog.com)** — Same login, same data. Use this for managing your account, generating reports, or testing on a computer. (Note: GPS trip tracking only works on the mobile app.)

### Creating an account

1. Open the app or go to **https://intracklog.com**
2. Tap **"Get started"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Full name** — used on your reports
   - **Email** — your login and where reset links are sent
   - **Password** — at least 6 characters, with at least one letter and one number
4. Tap **Continue**
5. (Optional) Add your first vehicle now or skip and add later
6. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to confirm your address

### Signing in

1. Open the app or website
2. Tap **"Sign in"**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Tap **Sign in** — you'll land on your dashboard

### Forgot your password?

1. On the sign in screen, tap **"Forgot password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (also check spam)
4. Click the link → you'll be taken to a page to choose a new password
5. Sign in with your new password

## 2. Your First Vehicle

Before you can log trips, you need at least one vehicle.

### Adding a vehicle

1. From the dashboard, tap **Vehicles** (the car icon)
2. Tap **+ Add vehicle**
3. Fill in:
   - **Licence plate number** — e.g. "CA 456-789"
   - **Description** — what the vehicle is, e.g. "Toyota Hilux 2.4 GD-6"
   - **Current mileage (km)** — your odometer reading right now
4. Tap **Add vehicle**

The vehicle appears on your dashboard. Tap it to view details, log trips, or update settings.

### Updating odometer readings

If your odometer creeps ahead of what's in the app:

1. Tap **Account** (top right) → **Settings** → **Odometer**
2. Find your vehicle and update the **Current reading**
3. The change is saved automatically

## 4. Tracking Trips

You can log trips two ways: **GPS tracking** (automatic) or **manual entry**.

### Method A: GPS tracking (mobile app only)

1. From the dashboard, tap **"Start a trip"**
2. If you have multiple vehicles, choose which one you're using
3. Allow location permissions when prompted
4. Drive! The app tracks your route automatically.
5. When done, tap **"Stop & save trip"**

The trip is saved with:
- Distance travelled
- Duration
- Start and end locations (auto-detected via GPS)
- Date and time

### Method B: Manual entry

1. Tap **Vehicles** → choose your vehicle
2. Fill in the **New entry** form:
   - **Date**
   - **Odometer start** and **Odometer end** (km)
   - **Expense type** — Fuel, Service, Tyres, Toll, or Other
   - **Amount (R)**
   - **Notes** (optional)
3. Attach receipts or photos if you have them
4. Tap **Save entry**

### When to use which?

| Scenario | Method |
|----------|--------|
| You're about to start driving | GPS — tap Start a trip |
| You forgot to start GPS | Manual entry |
| You don't have signal/permissions | Manual entry |
| You want a precise route map | GPS |

## 5. Logging Expenses

Every trip can have an expense attached, and you can log expenses without a trip too (e.g. servicing, tyres).

### Expense types

- **Fuel** — petrol, diesel
- **Service** — vehicle servicing
- **Tyres** — replacement, rotation, repair
- **Toll** — e-toll, gantry
- **Other** — anything else

### Attaching receipts

When logging an entry:
1. Scroll down to the **Attachments** section
2. Tap to add a photo from your camera or pick a file from your phone
3. The receipt is uploaded and stored with the entry

Your receipts are accessible later in Reports for SARS submissions.

## 6. Saving Locations

Frequently visit the same places? Save them as **locations** for quick reference and clearer reports.

### Adding a saved location

1. From the dashboard, tap **Locations**
2. Tap **+ Add a saved location**
3. Fill in:
   - **Location name** — e.g. "Head Office"
   - **Address**
   - **City**
   - **Country**
4. Tap **Save location**

### Auto-discovered locations

Anywhere you start or end a GPS trip is automatically detected and added under **"Discovered from trips"**. These help you see where you've been without manual setup.

## 7. Working with Teams

If you manage a fleet or work with colleagues who share a workplace, **Teams** lets you coordinate.

> **Note:** Teams is available on **Pro** and **Business** plans only.

### Creating a team

1. Tap **Teams** on the dashboard
2. Tap the dropdown → **+ Create a new team**
3. Fill in:
   - **Team name** — e.g. "Sales Team"
   - **Workplace name** — e.g. "Head Office"
   - **Workplace address**
4. Tap **Next**
5. Choose a **mileage rate**:
   - **Standard** — SARS rate of R 4.64/km
   - **Custom** — enter your own rate
   - **None** — no reimbursement rate
6. Tap **Create team** (or **Skip** to skip rate)

### Adding members

1. Inside your team, tap the **Members** tab
2. Tap **+ Add**
3. Enter the member's name, email, and role (Admin or Member)
4. Tap **Add member**

### Sending invitations

1. In the team view, tap the **Pending** tab
2. Tap **+ Send invite**
3. Enter the person's email
4. Tap **Send invite**

Your default email app opens with a pre-written invitation. Just hit Send. The pending invite appears in the Pending tab until they join or you cancel.

### Switching between teams

Use the dropdown at the top of the Teams section to switch between **My Logbook** (your personal trips), any team you belong to, or to create a new team.

## 8. Generating Reports

Reports are how you get your data out of the app for SARS submissions, employer reimbursement, or personal records.

### From the dashboard

1. Tap **Account** (top right) → **Settings** → **Generate Report**
2. You'll see a summary card showing:
   - Total trips
   - Total distance
   - Total expenses
3. Choose your export format:
   - **Download PDF report** — a printable, branded PDF (best for SARS / employer)
   - **Download CSV (Excel)** — opens in Excel/Google Sheets for further analysis
   - **Export all my data (JSON)** — full data export including teams, locations, settings (your POPIA right)

### What's in the PDF report

- **Header** — your name, email, employee number (if set), date generated
- **Summary table** — total trips, total km, mileage rate, reimbursement, expenses
- **Trip log** — every trip with date, vehicle, plate, from/to, distance, reimbursement amount
- **Footer note** — explains the report's intended use

### Sharing the report

After generating, the file is ready to share via:
- WhatsApp / email / Drive
- Print directly
- Send to your accountant

## 9. Subscriptions & Billing

InTrackLog has four plans:

| Plan | Price | Vehicles | Teams | Highlights |
|------|-------|----------|-------|-----------|
| **Free** | R 0/month | 3 | 0 | Manual logging, basic reports |
| **Starter** | R 75/month | 5 | 0 | + GPS tracking, expense tracking, full history |
| **Pro** | R 99/month | 10 | 1 | + Teams (5 members), custom rates, priority support |
| **Business** | R 249/month | Unlimited | Unlimited | + Admin dashboard, API access, dedicated support |

### Upgrading

1. Tap **Account** → **Edit Account** → **My Subscription** tab
2. Tap the plan you want
3. A payment form opens — fill in your card details
4. Tap **Pay R XX**
5. You'll be redirected to **PayFast** (South Africa's secure payment processor) to complete payment
6. After payment, you're returned to the app and your plan is upgraded

PayFast accepts:
- Credit / Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Instant EFT
- SnapScan / Capitec Pay
- Masterpass / Zapper

### Cancelling or downgrading

To switch back to Free or a lower plan:
1. Go to **Edit Account → My Subscription**
2. Tap the **Free** plan (or any lower one)
3. Confirm — your account downgrades immediately

PayFast subscriptions can also be cancelled directly through PayFast or by emailing support@intracklog.com.

## 10. Account & Settings

### Edit Account

Tap **Account → Edit Account**. Two tabs:

**My Subscription** — see and change your plan (covered above).

**Account Details** — edit:
- Full name
- Email address
- **Employee number** — appears on your generated reports for employer reimbursement
- **Change password** — enter your current password, then a new one

Plus you can see your Account ID, current plan, and vehicle count.

### Settings

Tap **Account → Settings**. Five sections:

1. **Vehicles** — view and add registered vehicles
2. **Workplace** — set your default workplace name and address (used in reports)
3. **Mileage Rate** — set the rate per km used to calculate reimbursement (default R 4.64 — the SARS standard rate)
4. **Odometer** — update each vehicle's current odometer reading
5. **Notifications** — toggle reminders (see next section)
6. **Generate Report** — covered above

## 11. Notifications

The app can send you reminder notifications:

### Setting up

1. Tap **Account → Settings → Notifications**
2. Toggle on what you want:
   - **Daily trip reminder** — 6:00 PM every day, prompts you to log today's trips
   - **Monthly report reminder** — 1st of every month, prompts you to generate your report
3. Tap **Send test notification** to confirm they're working

### Disabling

Toggle them off in the same place. You can also disable all notifications from your device's system settings.

## 12. Privacy, Security & Data

### Your data is yours

- **All your data is stored securely** in our cloud database (Supabase, encrypted in transit and at rest)
- **Only you can access your data** thanks to row-level security
- **GPS tracking is opt-in** and only runs when you actively start a trip
- **We never sell your data** to third parties

### Reading the legal stuff

- **Privacy Policy** — Account dropdown → Privacy Policy. Explains what data we collect, how we use it, your POPIA rights, and how to contact us.
- **Terms of Service** — Account dropdown → Terms of Service. The legal terms of using InTrackLog.

### Exporting your data

Per POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act), you have the right to receive all data we hold about you:

1. **Account → Settings → Generate Report**
2. Tap **Export all my data (JSON)**
3. The file contains everything: vehicles, trips, expenses, locations, teams, settings

### Deleting your account

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

- Email **privacy@intracklog.com** with subject "Account deletion request"
- We'll remove your data within 30 days
- Some data may be retained as required by law (SARS records: 5 years)

### Changing your password

You can change your password anytime:
- **From the app**: Account → Edit Account → Account Details → Change password
- **If you've forgotten it**: Use the "Forgot password?" link on the sign in screen

## 13. Troubleshooting

### "GPS tracking won't start"

- Check that location permissions are granted: Phone Settings → Apps → InTrackLog → Permissions → Location → Allow
- GPS only works on the **mobile app**, not the website
- Restart the app after granting permissions

### "I added a vehicle but it didn't save"

- Make sure your subscription tier supports another vehicle (Free = 3, Starter = 5, Pro = 10, Business = unlimited)
- Check your internet connection
- Try logging out and back in

### "The Pay button does nothing on the website"

- Make sure you're using **https://intracklog.com** (the secure version)
- Some browsers block payment forms on insecure connections
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)

### "I'm not getting password reset emails"

- Check your **spam / junk folder**
- Confirm you typed the right email address
- Wait a few minutes — emails can take up to 5 minutes
- Try again — request a new reset link

### "The app is on Trial mode but I paid"

If your subscription tier didn't update after payment:
- Wait 1-2 minutes for PayFast to confirm the transaction
- Refresh the app or sign out and back in
- If still wrong, email support@intracklog.com with your transaction reference

### "I can't see my teams"

- Teams require a **Pro or Business** subscription
- Check Account → Edit Account → My Subscription to confirm your tier
